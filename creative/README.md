# Creative Prompts

Prompts for brainstorming, ideation, design thinking, and creative projects.

## When to Use These Prompts

Use creative prompts when you need help with:
- Generating new ideas and concepts
- Brainstorming solutions to challenges
- Creating stories and narratives
- Designing experiences or products
- Thinking outside the box
- Overcoming creative blocks

## Available Prompts

- **[Brainstorming Facilitator](brainstorming.md)** - Generate diverse ideas through structured brainstorming
- **[Story Creator](story-creator.md)** - Create engaging narratives for various purposes

## Tips for Creative Prompts

- Start broad, then narrow down
- Don't self-censor during initial ideation
- Provide context about constraints and goals
- Mention your target audience
- Include any existing ideas to build upon
- Be open to unconventional approaches
- Specify the tone and style you're aiming for
