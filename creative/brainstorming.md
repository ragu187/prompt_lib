# Brainstorming Facilitator

## Category
Creative

## Description
Generate creative ideas and solutions through structured brainstorming, exploring multiple perspectives and possibilities.

## Prompt

```
Let's brainstorm ideas for:

**Topic/Challenge:**
[TOPIC]

**Goal:**
[OBJECTIVE]

**Target Audience/Users:**
[AUDIENCE]

**Constraints:**
- [CONSTRAINT_1]
- [CONSTRAINT_2]

**Current Ideas (if any):**
[EXISTING_IDEAS]

Please generate:
1. **Diverse Ideas**: 10-15 creative ideas ranging from practical to innovative
2. **Categorization**: Group ideas by theme or approach
3. **Top Picks**: Highlight 3-5 most promising ideas with reasoning
4. **Combinations**: Suggest ways to combine ideas for stronger solutions
5. **Next Steps**: How to evaluate and develop these ideas further

Think outside the box and explore unconventional approaches!
```

## Variables

- `[TOPIC]`: The subject or challenge for brainstorming
- `[OBJECTIVE]`: What you want to achieve
- `[AUDIENCE]`: Who the ideas are for
- `[CONSTRAINT_1/2]`: Any limitations or requirements
- `[EXISTING_IDEAS]`: Ideas already considered

## Example Usage

### Input
```
Let's brainstorm ideas for:

**Topic/Challenge:**
Increasing employee engagement in remote team

**Goal:**
Improve team connection and collaboration while working remotely

**Target Audience/Users:**
Software development team of 25 people across 3 time zones

**Constraints:**
- Limited budget ($2000)
- Must work across time zones
- Should not require significant time commitment

**Current Ideas (if any):**
- Weekly video calls
- Slack channels for casual chat

Please generate:
1. **Diverse Ideas**: 10-15 creative ideas ranging from practical to innovative
2. **Categorization**: Group ideas by theme or approach
3. **Top Picks**: Highlight 3-5 most promising ideas with reasoning
4. **Combinations**: Suggest ways to combine ideas for stronger solutions
5. **Next Steps**: How to evaluate and develop these ideas further

Think outside the box and explore unconventional approaches!
```

### Expected Output
A comprehensive list of creative ideas including virtual events, async collaboration tools, recognition systems, etc., organized by category with actionable recommendations.

## Tips

- Don't self-censor during initial brainstorming
- Encourage quantity over quality at first
- Build on existing ideas rather than dismissing them
- Consider perspectives from different stakeholders
- Mix practical and ambitious ideas
- Can be used for products, processes, marketing, problem-solving
